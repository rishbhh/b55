ONE STOP SHOP FOR IRON AND STEEL

The well established iron and steel company

Founded in 2002 by its CEO Mukesh V. Thakwani, fondly known as Mr.Mike, B5 Plus Ltd has its Headquarters in Ghana. B5 Plus Ltd has a conglomerate of business operations spread across different countries in West Africa.

B5 Plus Limited manufactures and trades in well-finished iron and steel products which are known for their high quality. The unbeatable spirit of this iron and steel company stays buoyed up by the relentless efforts of our employees under the astute guidance of our CEO who has been steering this company in the path of excellence since its inception.

Vision

core values

Our People

“EVERY PERSON MATTERS” B5 Plus believes in the passion and dedication of each employee. We see the good in people and try to develop these qualities, i.e. preparing and grooming the next generation of young thinkers.

Integrity

We are each personally accountable for the highest standard of behaviour which include honesty and fairness in all aspect of our work. We fulfil our commitments as responsible citizens, employees and teammates. We will consistently treat customers and company resources with respect.

Leadership

B5 Plus leadership is founded on talented people effectively applying common sense, stewardship and sound business management. We will lead through our competence, creativity and teamwork. By demonstrating the company’s values and ethics on a daily basis, our leaders set the tone for consistent behaviour and successful performance. Our leaders encourage clear, two-way communication, and foster a positive work environment that encourages growth.

Safety

Safety is our chosen lifestyle. We embrace safety as an obligation. We are committed to the well-being, self-preservation and personal survival of every employee. We are our brother’s keeper for whom we will not falter or fail. Safety is a function of our normal daily activities and we shall endeavour to uphold personal awareness to the highest degree of excellence. We will continuously provide our employees with the best training available and demand their personal commitment to this cause.
