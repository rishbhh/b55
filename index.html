about us

Head quartered at Tema in Ghana, B5Plus is one of the largest iron and steel companytrading in all over the West African Countries. Founded in 2002, B5 Plus Ltd has a conglomerate of business operations spread across different countries in West Africa.

Today B5 Plus group is a market leader in Iron rods, Various steel products, PEB structures, Chemicals, etc.

Today, B5 Plus Group directly and indirectly employs over Ten (10) Thousand people.
